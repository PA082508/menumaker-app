# SafePass — Red Room, Monday

## What today is for

We are testing the screen and the routine, not you. The paper sign-in sheet
stays the official record for the day. **Keep filling it in exactly as you do
now** — the tablet does not replace it yet.

## 1. The tablet

One iPad lives in Red. It is already set up. Do not sign out of it, and do not
open the app on your own phone.

If the screen shows a red bar saying the device is not registered — **stop, do
not improvise, call Sonia.** The Accept / Release buttons will be greyed out on
purpose in that case.

## 2. A parent arrives

The parent starts the handoff on their side. Their request appears in the
**left-hand queue** on the tablet, with the child's name and who is collecting.

Check the person in front of you first, the screen second. **The screen never
overrides your eyes.** If you do not recognise the person, do not release the
child — call Sonia.

## 3A. Confirming — WITH PIN  *(keep this version if the new path is live)*

1. Tap **✓ Accept** (drop-off) or **✓ Release** (pick-up) on that child's card.
2. A keypad appears. **Type your own 4-digit PIN.** Yours, not a shared one —
   the system records who took the child, and that is the whole point.
3. The card moves to today's log with your name on it.

**If the PIN is refused:** you get 4 tries, then the pad locks for 45 seconds.
Wait it out — it will unlock by itself. Do not keep tapping, and do not use the
other teacher's PIN. If it still refuses after the lock clears, use the paper
sheet and call Sonia.

## 3B. Confirming — NO PIN  *(keep this version if the new path is not live)*

1. Tap **✓ Accept** (drop-off) or **✓ Release** (pick-up) on that child's card.
2. The card moves to today's log.

There is no PIN step today. The system will not record which of you confirmed —
so **write your initials on the paper sheet** for every child. That is the only
record of who took the child today.

## 4. If something goes wrong

| What you see | What to do |
|---|---|
| Nothing happens when you tap | Wait 5 seconds, tap once more. Do not tap repeatedly — a double tap is safe, but ten taps make it hard to read the log afterwards. |
| Red bar / "device not registered" | Stop. Paper sheet. Call Sonia. |
| **No WiFi / nothing loads** | The tablet does **not** work without WiFi today. Go to the paper sheet immediately and call Sonia. Do not wait for it to come back. |
| A name is missing from the list | Paper sheet, and tell Sonia at the end of the morning — do not confirm a different child. |
| Anything you are unsure about | Paper sheet. It is always the safe answer. |

**The paper sheet is never the wrong choice.** If the tablet and the paper
disagree, the paper wins.

## 5. Who to call

**Sonia Texidor Rosa — Administrator.** Call, do not text, if a child is
standing in front of you.

## 6. At the end of the morning

Tell Sonia, in your own words:

- anything that was confusing or slower than the paper sheet;
- anything the screen said that did not match what was happening;
- anything you wanted to do and could not find.

That list is the actual output of the rehearsal — more useful to us than a
