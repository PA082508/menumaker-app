1
Program Type

This is the master switch for the entire application. It controls which sidebar sections, reports, and forms are available to your team. All existing data — menus, recipes, children — is preserved when you switch.

OptionWhen to chooseSections added to sidebar
CACFP Your center participates in the Child and Adult Care Food Program and submits monthly reimbursement claims Claim Report, CACFP Reports
Head Start Your center is funded by a Head Start or Early Head Start grant and operates under federal standards 45 CFR Part 1302 Family Engagement, HS Reports; RD approval workflow in Menu Planner
What changes when you select Head Start:
• Menu Planner shows a status bar: Draft → Submitted for RD Review → RD Approved → Published
• Special Diet forms gain CF/HS-27 fields (substitution type, review date, Health Manager sign-off)
• Recipes gain a Product Documents panel (upload CN Labels, Standardized Recipe cards, Product Specs)
• HS Reports provides PIR nutrition section data ready to enter into HSES
2
Program Schedule
⏱ Daily Hours
The total number of hours children are enrolled in the program each day. Head Start Performance Standards set different meal nutrition requirements based on this value:
Program lengthMeal nutrition requirement
Less than 6 hours Meals must provide ⅓ to ½ of children's daily nutritional needs
6 hours or more Meals must provide ½ to ⅔ of children's daily nutritional needs
Example: 6.5 for a program running 7:00 AM – 1:30 PM
The app displays a live hint below the field as soon as you type a value — you do not need to memorize the threshold.
🕐 Start Time / End Time
The actual opening and closing time of your program each day. Use 24-hour HH:MM format. These times appear in reports and documentation to confirm the length of the program day.
Start Time: 07:00
End Time: 13:30
📅 Fiscal Year Start Month
The month your grant-funded fiscal year begins. This controls the reporting period displayed in HS Reports → PIR Data.
Default is October — the federal fiscal year in the U.S. begins October 1. If your grant starts in a different month, check your Notice of Award from HHS.
3
Registered Dietitian (RD)

Under 45 CFR 1302.44(b), all Head Start menus must be planned or approved by a licensed nutrition professional. The information in this section appears in the Menu Planner approval workflow and is printed on menus distributed to families.

Name
Full name of the dietitian who approves menus. Recorded in the database when Approve Menu (RD) is clicked in the Menu Planner, and printed on menu handouts sent to families.
Example: Sarah Johnson
Credentials
Professional credential abbreviations, comma-separated. Displayed next to the dietitian's name on reports and menu printouts to confirm their qualifications.
Common:RD, LDN
RD, CDN, MS
RD, LD
Email
The dietitian's professional email address. Used to identify the approver in the menu workflow. In a future release, this address will receive automatic notifications when a menu is submitted for review.
Example: s.johnson@nutritionservices.com
Grant Number
Your Head Start grant number from the Notice of Award issued by HHS. Included in PIR reports and all official nutrition documentation. You can find it on the first page of your grant agreement.
Example:90CI5432/01
06CH010123
4
Health Manager

The Health Services Manager is responsible for reviewing and authorizing special dietary accommodations. Their name appears on CF/HS-27 forms in the Form Submissions section as the sign-off authority.

Name
Full name of the Health Services Manager. Displayed in Form Submissions → Special Diet / CF-HS27 under the Health Manager Sign-Off field for each accommodation on file.
Example: Maria Gonzalez
Email
Professional email of the Health Manager. Used for notifications when new special diet forms are submitted and require review.
Enrollment Capacity
The maximum number of children your program is licensed to serve. Used in HS Reports → PIR Data as the denominator when calculating the percentage of enrolled children with documented dietary needs.
Example: 120
5
Saving Your Changes

All four sections — Program Type, Schedule, Registered Dietitian, and Health Manager — are saved together with a single click of Save Changes at the bottom of the page.

  • Click Save Changes — a green "Saved" confirmation appears when successful
  • The sidebar navigation updates automatically after saving — no page reload needed
  • If a red error message appears, check your internet connection and try again
  • You can return to this page at any time to update individual fields — changes take effect immediately
