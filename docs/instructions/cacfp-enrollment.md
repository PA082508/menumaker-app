# CACFP Enrollment Form

Official Ohio DEW (Office of Nutrition) form, revision 8/2025. Required for every enrolled child at CACFP child care centers. Our interactive version lives in the parent packet:

**Form:** `forms/1-data-sources/CACFP_Enrollment_v6.html`

## When this form is required

- One form per child. Siblings each need their own form — their attendance may differ.
- The form must be completed **entirely by the parent/guardian**: days, hours, and meals.
- Valid for **12 months following the month the parent dated it**. Example: signed 7/13/2026 → expires 7/31/2027. The toolbar shows the expiration date automatically after the signature date is entered.
- Must be renewed **annually**.
- Hand it out **together with the Income Eligibility Application** so both documents expire at the same time.
- Not required for: Outside-School Hours Centers, Youth Development Programs, After School at Risk Programs, Emergency Shelters (not our case — all three Play Academy centers require it).

## Completing online

1. Open the form. Select the center in the toolbar — the CENTER NAME field fills in automatically.
2. The parent completes the schedule grid: check the days the child normally attends, pick arrive/depart times from the dropdowns (both morning and afternoon columns if the child comes before and after school), and check the meals received on each day.
3. If the parent's work schedule changes frequently, check the box **"Yes, the schedule listed above may frequently vary"**. With this box checked, a new form is not required when attendance shifts.
4. The parent signs in the yellow signature box (finger on a tablet/phone, mouse on a computer). The date defaults to today. Fill in day phone, mailing address, parent birthdate, and parent email.
5. Click **Submit**. A green confirmation appears: the submission is stored as **pending** (child_id NEW-...) until the center reviews and approves it in the Enrollment Inbox. No Child ID is needed — parents just fill and submit.

## Printing

- **Filled form:** complete the fields, then click **Print**. The toolbar and yellow highlights disappear — the printout is the clean official form with the entered values.
- **Blank form:** click **Clear**, then **Print** — a blank state form for wet signature.
- Print settings: Letter, scale 100%.

## Common issues

- **"Select center first"** — the center dropdown at the top must be chosen before Submit.
- **Signature not saving** — the signature box must contain actual strokes; an empty box saves as no signature.
- **Wrong expiration date** — the date is derived from the parent's signature date, not today's date. Correct the DATE field and Submit again.

## Compliance notes

- CACFP federal regulation 226.15(e)(2) requires an annually completed, parent-signed enrollment form on file.
- Keep the signed form (electronic record or printed wet-signature copy) available for state review.
