# Photos — Children & Staff

One photo per person, used everywhere. Take it once and it appears on the roster, in
Children, in Meal Count and in SafePass — automatically. There is no second copy to
keep in sync, because there is no second copy.

## Where to take a photo

**A child** — Children → open the classroom → tap the child → **Profile** tab → **Photo**.

**A staff member** — Staff → open the person → **Profile** tab → **Photo**.

## Taking it

Tap the round photo (or the **Add photo** / **Change photo** button). Three choices appear:

| | |
|---|---|
| **📷 Take photo** | Opens the camera right away. On a child's card it opens the **rear** camera; on a staff card it opens the **front** camera, so a staff member can photograph themselves. |
| **🖼 Choose from library** | Pick a picture already on the iPad or computer. |
| **🗑 Remove photo** | Clears the photo. The person shows their coloured initials again. |

Then press **✓ Save** on a child's card, or **Save Changes** on a staff card.
**The photo is not on the record until you save.** If you close the card without
saving, the photo is not kept.

## Tips for a photo that reads well

- **Face fills the circle.** The picture is cropped to a square and shrunk to 512
  pixels — a full-body shot at arm's length becomes an unrecognisable dot.
- **Face the light**, don't stand with a window behind you.
- **Plain wall behind** the child if you can. Busy backgrounds fight the face at small sizes.
- **One child per photo.** The circle is small; two faces means neither is usable.
- Take it again any time — a new photo simply replaces the old one.

## If it doesn't save

You will see a red line: **"Photo not saved — …"**. That is the real answer, not a
glitch to retry blindly. It usually means your account isn't allowed to change photos
for that person. Nothing was written — the old photo is untouched. Send the wording to
the office rather than trying again from another device.

If the photo looks unchanged after saving, close the card and reopen it. If it is
still the old one, it did not save — tell the office.

## Privacy

- Photos live in a **private** store. They are not on the public internet, they have no
  shareable link, and a link that a browser holds expires within the hour.
- Only signed-in staff of this organisation can see them. Parents cannot browse them.
- **Consent is already covered** by the documents families sign at enrolment — you do
  not need to collect anything extra before taking a child's photo.
- Photos are for identifying a child at drop-off, pick-up and meal counts. Don't put
  them anywhere else, and don't photograph a child's paperwork into this field.
- **Removing a photo** clears it from every screen at once. That is the right action if
  a family asks.

## Good to know

- **Only one photo per person** — a new photo replaces the previous one everywhere. There
  is no gallery and no history.
- The photo you take on a child's card is the same photo the roster and SafePass show.
  You never have to add it twice.
