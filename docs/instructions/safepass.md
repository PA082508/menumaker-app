# SafePass

SafePass records the **chain of custody** for every child — drop-off, pick-up,
early/late care, and transport. Each tap is a legal timestamp, so the rule behind
everything is **physical handoff only**: responsibility transfers when the child is
physically in your hands (Accept) or in the authorized person's hands (Release) —
never when a parent merely enters the building.

## Regular drop-off & pick-up

1. **Select your classroom** — you see only children enrolled in your class.
2. **Wait for incoming requests** — when a parent taps *Drop Off*/*Pick Up*, the child appears in **Incoming Requests**.
3. **Physically receive the child** — take the child into your care first.
4. **Tap ACCEPT** — creates the legal timestamp; the parent gets ✅.
5. **Pick-up: verify the person** — confirm they're **Authorized**. Don't recognize them? Request photo ID. Not authorized → **do not release**, call your director.
6. **Hand over physically** — place the child in the authorized person's hands.
7. **Tap RELEASE** — the parent gets confirmation; the handoff is recorded.

## Early Care

1. **Switch to ☀️ Early Care** — shows children from **all** classrooms who arrived before their Lead Teacher.
2. **Receive each child** — Accept normally; each shows a waiting timer.
3. **Monitor the ratio** — Ohio minimums apply, set by the youngest child present. Yellow = warning, Red = over limit.
4. **Transfer to class** — when a Lead Teacher arrives, tap *Transfer to Class* for their children.
5. **Escalate if needed** — 15+ min → *Call*; 45+ min → *CPS* and notify your director.

## Late Care

1. **Switch to 🌙 Late Care** — after closing time with children remaining.
2. **Shift cannot close** — the system blocks close while children are present; you're responsible for every child on screen.
3. **15 min — call parent** — tap 📞 *Call* to log it.
4. **30 min — alert director** — tap 📋 *Director*.
5. **45 min — emergency contacts** — tap 🚨 *911*.
6. **60 min — CPS** — tap 🚨 *CPS*; stay with the child.
7. **Parent arrives** — tap ✓ *Parent Arrived*, verify identity, hand over physically.

## Transport (school-age bus runs)

1. **Switch to 🚌 Transport** — today's runs.
2. **Point 1 — Center → Driver** — check each name before boarding, tap *Boarded*.
3. **Point 2 — Driver → School** — driver completes the checklist with GPS timestamp; Play Academy responsibility ends at the school's drop-off point.
4. **Point 3 — School → Driver** — school staff confirms children boarding the return vehicle.
5. **Point 4 — Driver → Center** — confirm each child returned; tap ✓ *Confirm All Children Returned*.

## Rules

- **Physical handoff only** — transfer happens at the handoff, not at building entry.
- **Never tap in advance** — your tap is your legal signature.
- **Unrecognized persons** — request a government photo ID; not on the authorized list → don't release, call your director.
- **Unknown phone alert** — an unregistered device attempting pick-up → keep the child with you, contact your director.
- **Ratio compliance** — watch your count; Early Care uses Ohio minimums for the youngest child present.
- **Shift close** — you can't close your shift while any child is present.
