# E-Forms — Teacher Checklist

Five things, for when a parent asks you at the door.

1. **You do not send packets.** Enrollment forms come from the director — send the
   parent to the office rather than forwarding a link yourself.
2. **A parent can fill everything on their phone.** No paper, no printing, no
   appointment. It takes most families about ten minutes.
3. **They sign once.** The first form is a Consent; after that every form offers a
   "✍️ Use my signature" button. If a parent says they are being asked to sign
   repeatedly, something is wrong — tell the director.
4. **If a link says "open this packet from your center's link"** — that link lost its
   center. The director sends a fresh one; it is not the parent's fault.
5. **You are a parent here too?** Your child's forms and your employment forms are
   separate, with separate signatures, even on the same tablet. Signing one never
   fills the other.
